George Foreman Mix Go

George Foreman Mix Go is an Australian blender. We acquired our stock through NZ retailers then went on to sell the blender directly our Chinese customers. We ran several promotions on our social channels to introduce this product and generate interest in the Chinese market. Our customer service team spend a lot of time to answer customers questions and get feedback as well. From 2015 to 2016, it was one of our top selling products. We sold more than 300 blenders a month for a number of months.

We faced two challenges with selling the George Foreman Mix Go. Firstly, we were always out of stock and secondly, we had problems with the product quality. Customers started complaining about the faulty product which was leaking after only several uses. Since customers cannot post back to New Zealand for the product to be fixed with the high shipping cost.

We came to the conclusion that a simple solution is to post a lid to customers. Unfortunately, we weren't able to get hold of the company to let them know the issues and that we're happy to buy the parts and take the cost of shipping fees to satisfy our customers. However, the company's sales manager wasn't taking the issue seriously. After 2016, none of our customers wanted the blender because of the reviews etc so we decided to no longer stock the product.

This is an example of a failed case to show that a popular product can go wrong when communication channels are blocked.

Bio Active Goats Milk

A lot of Asian consumers have problems to digesting milk. Because of this, alternative product's that are claimed to be easy to digest like Goats milk have become popular sellers. Additionally, it's common Chinese believe that goats’ milk is better than cow milk. During a few months’ promotion, we achieved top sales through Taobao (China's No1 e-commerce platform). Unlike the George Foreman company, BioActive communicated well with us. They invited Ronglan (our CEO) to visit their office. Two days later Ronglan and the BioActive CEO, and founder all met. We've learnt about product features and opportunities from BioActive and they also get more feedback from us. We are supporting each other to satisfy our customers. Our BioActive goat milk sales continue to grow.

8 minutes Facial Mask

Understanding customers' needs and pain points is very important. Most Chinese women prefer to use sheet masks rather than other types of facial mask like cream, gel etc. 8 minutes Facial Mask was created as the first New Zealand sheet facial mask they've only launched this year and we sold more than 900 boxes in the first week of the product launch.

Traditional Healthy Products

Chinese diets are becoming healthier as their education and income situation improves. Many middle-class Chinese consumers care about health and prefer to buy foreign healthy food brands. Traditional New Zealand healthy and nutritious food is booming in China today. As a result, we are selling more than 200 boxes of products like Weet-bix each month.
