Case Study

George Foreman Mix Go

George Foreman Mix Go is an Australian blender. It has good price and reasonable quality. We have done our promotion on our social channels to introduce this product. Our customer service team spend a lot of time to answer customers questions and get feedback as well. In 2015 to 2016, it was our top selling items. We have bought all the stocks from all Auckland retail shops and directly sold them to our Chinese customers. We can sell more than 300 blender each month. We also face two challenges. First one is that we always run out the stock. Second one is the quality. Some users used it less than one year and it start leaking. Customers cannot post back New Zealand to get fixing as the high shipping cost. The simple solution is to post a lid to customers but this parts cannot be sold separately. We want to discuss with this company let them know the issues and we are happy to buy the parts and take the cost of shipping fees to satisfy our customers. However, we would not have a chance to meet with this company's sales manager even we try all we can do. After 2016, none of our customers are willing to buy this product. This is a failure case that a popular product can go bad when communication channels are blocked.

Bio Active Goats Milk

A lot of Asian have problems to digest milk. The goats milk is another alternative product for them. In addition, Chinese believe that goats milk is better than cow milk. During a few month promotion, we achieved the top sale in Taobao (China's No1 e-commerce platform). Unlike George Foreman company, Bio Active company noticed us. They invited Ronglan (our CEO) to visit their office. Two days later, Ronglan, Bio Active CEO, and founder meet together. We learn more product features and opportunities from Bio Active and they also get more feedback from us. We are supporting each other to satisfy our customers. Our goat milk sales level continues to grow.

8 minutes Facial Mask

Understanding customers needs and pain points is very important. Most Chinese woman prefer to use sheet mask than other type of facial mask like cream, gel etc. 8 minutes Facial Mask is created as the first New Zealand sheet facial mask in this year. We sold more than 900 boxes in first week.

Traditional Healthy Products

Nowadays, Chinese consumption level has significant improve as the education and income increasing. Many middle class of Chinese more care about health than past 5 years and prefer to buy foreign healthy food brands. Traditional New Zealand healthy food is booming in China. As the results, we are selling more than 200 boxes each month.
